Weight-Based Dialysis Calculator

Includes (1) HD UF range by UFR band and (2) CRRT effluent dose targets.

1) Intermittent HD: UF Range

Uses UFR band 10–13 mL/kg/hr to estimate UF volume range.
Formula: UF (mL) = Weight (kg) × Duration (hr) × UFR (mL/kg/hr).

2) CRRT: Effluent Dose Targets

Outputs target effluent rate for 20 / 25 / 30 mL/kg/hr mL/hr L/day
Effluent rate (mL/hr) = Weight (kg) × Dose (mL/kg/hr). L/day = (mL/hr × 24) / 1000.
